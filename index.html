Rules

Basic Rules of Texas Hold'em

  1. Blinds: The game starts with two players putting in small and big blinds.
  2. Dealing Cards: Each player is dealt two private cards.
  3. Betting Rounds: There are several betting rounds: pre-flop, flop, turn, and river.
  4. The Flop: Three community cards are dealt face-up on the table.
  5. The Turn: A fourth community card is dealt face-up.
  6. The River: A fifth and final community card is dealt face-up.
  7. Showdown: Players reveal their hands, and the best five-card hand wins.

Hand Rankings

Poker Hand Rankings

  • Royal Flush: A, K, Q, J, 10, all of the same suit.

  • Straight Flush: Five cards in a sequence, all of the same suit.

  • Four of a Kind: Four cards of the same rank.

  • Full House: Three of a kind with a pair.

  • Flush: Five cards of the same suit, not in sequence.

  • Straight: Five cards in a sequence, not of the same suit.

  • Three of a Kind: Three cards of the same rank.

  • Two Pair: Two different pairs.

  • One Pair: Two cards of the same rank.

  • High Card: The highest card wins if no one has a pair or better.
